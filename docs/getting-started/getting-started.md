# Getting Started with Choro

Welcome! Get up and running with Choro in minutes.

---

## Step 1: Log In to Your Account

Start by logging into Choro with your preferred method.

![Login Page](/docs/images/login.png)

### Sign In Options:
- **Google** - Use your Google account
- **Meta (Facebook)** - Sign in with Facebook
- **Email** - Use your email and password

**New user?** Click **Sign up** to create your account.

---

## Step 2: Create a New Design

Click **New Chat** to start creating your design.

![Prompting](/docs/images/prompting.png)

### Describe Your Design

In the prompt box, describe what you want to create. Be as detailed as possible:

**Example prompt:**
```
Fashion promotion with 'AUTUMN COLLECTION' in large minimalist black font over beige background. Model wearing fall outfit centered. 'Available Now' in smaller text at bottom. Add gold geometric accents for luxury feel.
```

**Tips for better results:**
- Be specific about colors, style, and layout
- Mention text you want included
- Describe the mood or feeling
- Add details about positioning

## Step 3: Generation in Progress

Choro's AI is creating your design. This typically takes just a few seconds.

![Loading](/docs/images/loading.png)

**Average generation time:** 10-20 seconds

---

## Step 4: Review Your Results

Your designs are ready! Choro generates multiple variations for you to choose from.

![Sample Output](/docs/images/sample-output.png)

### What You Can Do:

- **Preview** - View each design in full resolution
- **Download** - Save your favorites
- **Regenerate** - Try different variations
- **Refine** - Adjust your prompt and try again

### Multiple Variations

Choro creates 4 different variations of your design, giving you options to choose the perfect one for your needs.

---

## Quick Tips

### Write Better Prompts
- **Be specific**: "Minimalist tech logo" vs "Logo"
- **Include details**: Colors, fonts, style, mood
- **Mention text**: If you want specific words included
- **Describe layout**: Where elements should be positioned

### Generation Speed
- Simple designs: 3-5 seconds
- Complex designs: 5-10 seconds
- Multiple variations: Generated simultaneously

### Save Your Work
All generated designs are automatically saved to your conversation history. Access them anytime from the sidebar.
